Our mission

Clear, culturally aware guidance when you need it

AfriSense was built for African communities to help people interpret symptoms without fear. We work with everyday language and emphasize safe next steps in low-resource settings. The tool does not diagnose or prescribe medication.

Medical disclaimer

AfriSense provides possible causes and risk guidance only. It is not a medical device and does not replace a qualified healthcare worker. If you have severe pain, trouble breathing, heavy bleeding, seizures, or sudden weakness, seek emergency care immediately.

Data & privacy

Sessions are anonymous. We store your chats locally in your browser. Optional Firestore storage is disabled by default. You can reset the session anytime from the History page.

Safety behavior

The AI is trained to never diagnose diseases or prescribe medication. It only suggests possible causes and supportive next steps.

Local relevance

Guidance includes clinics, pharmacies, CHPS compounds, and community support, suited for low-resource environments.

Offline ready

The UI works offline and caches core pages. AI responses require a data connection.
